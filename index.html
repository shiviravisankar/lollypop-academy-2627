AI. It's everywhere, right? Feels like the word's been thrown around so much it barely means anything anymore.

This 4-month UX cohort cuts through the noise. We focus on strong design basics first — how to think, see, and solve problems — and then explore how AI can actually support that process. You'll learn research, design systems, writing, storytelling, and more — all while understanding how to work with AI, not be replaced by it.

Then comes a 2-month internship where you apply everything to real projects and real challenges.
